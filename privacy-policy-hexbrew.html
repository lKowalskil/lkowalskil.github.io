Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hexbrew: Idle Alchemy Witch (the “App” or “Game”), published by Nyxelium (“we”, “us”, “our”), handles information when you play. Hexbrew is a cozy-gothic offline idle apothecary game available on Google Play and the Apple App Store (package / bundle ID com.nyxelium.hexbrew).

The short version
  • Your game save, progress and settings are stored only on your device. We don’t run a server that collects them, and we can’t see them.
  • There is no account and no login.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The Game is free and ad-supported. Third-party advertising (Google AdMob) and analytics/crash reporting (Google Firebase) collect device and usage data as described below.
  • Optional in-app purchases are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store — we never see your card or payment details.
  • You can control ad personalization through the on-screen consent prompt and your device settings.

1. Information stored on your device

Hexbrew is designed to work offline. The following stays on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Game save & progress — your stations, garden plots, discovered recipes, brews, familiars, currencies (Coin, Renown, Arcana), prestige progress and overall progression.
  • Preferences — chosen language, sound, notifications and other in-game settings, plus your tutorial progress.

This data is held locally by the app (via your device’s standard app storage) and is removed when you delete the App or clear its storage. The Game also provides an option to wipe your save from within its settings. Because this data never leaves your device, we cannot retrieve, restore or transfer it for you.

2. Information collected by third parties

To offer a free, ad-supported game and to keep it stable, we use the trusted third-party services below. These services may collect information directly from your device under their own privacy policies.

3. In-app purchases

Hexbrew offers optional in-app purchases (for example, removing interstitial and banner ads, cosmetic skins, and convenience boosts). No content is ever locked behind a purchase — the Game is fully playable for free.

  • Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing or the Apple App Store. We never receive or store your card number or other payment details.
  • The stores share limited transaction information with us (such as the product purchased and whether it completed) so we can deliver your item and support Restore Purchases.
  • Your purchase history is handled under the store’s own terms — see Google’s and Apple’s privacy policies.

4. How we use information

  • To provide, run and improve the Game.
  • To display and measure advertising, including optional rewarded ads you choose to watch for in-game bonuses.
  • To deliver in-app purchases and let you restore previous purchases.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability, performance and features.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

5. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where the EU/UK GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Consent — for personalized ads and, on iOS, for any tracking that uses your device’s advertising identifier. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — for non-personalized ads, usage analytics, crash and stability diagnostics, security and fraud prevention, balanced against your rights.
  • Performance of a contract — to process and restore in-app purchases you request.

6. Advertising & your choices

Hexbrew shows ads via Google AdMob. Rewarded ads are always optional. Depending on your region, platform and consent:

  • EEA, UK & Switzerland: a consent message (Google’s User Messaging Platform) is shown before personalized ads are served.
  • Apple (iOS/iPadOS): if we ask to use your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized ads, iOS shows Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt first. If you decline, you still see ads, but they are non-personalized.
  • California & other US states: you can opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of your data for personalized advertising through the in-app consent controls and your device settings.
  • Reset or limit your Advertising ID: Android → Settings → Privacy → Ads (or Google → Ads); iOS → Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and Apple Advertising.

Learn how Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

7. Device permissions & notifications

  • Notifications — optional local reminders that let you know when offline ingredients or brews have piled up and it’s worth returning. These are scheduled on your device; no message content is sent to or from a server. You can disable them at any time in your device settings, and Hexbrew reschedules pending reminders after your device restarts.
  • Internet access — used to load ads and to send the analytics and crash diagnostics described above.

Hexbrew does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos or files.

8. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Information is processed only by the service providers named in §2 and the app stores named in §3, acting on our behalf or as independent controllers under their own policies, and where required by law.

9. Data retention

On-device data persists until you delete it, wipe your save, or uninstall the App. Data held by Google and Apple services (advertising, analytics, crash reporting, purchases) is retained according to their respective policies. Crash and analytics data is typically retained for a limited period and in aggregated form.

10. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, California under the CCPA/CPRA, and similar laws elsewhere), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, to object to processing, to data portability, to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Because your game data lives only on your device, you can delete it directly by wiping your save, clearing the App’s storage, or uninstalling. For data held by Google or Apple services, see their policies. To make a request to us, contact us at the address below.

11. Children’s privacy

Hexbrew is intended for a general, adult-casual audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Ads are configured for a general audience; if you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us so we can address it.

12. International data transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than yours, including the United States. Where required, such transfers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses).

13. Security

We rely on your device’s app-sandboxing to protect on-device data and on reputable providers (Google, Apple) for processed data. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, when we add or change a third-party service. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the App or on this page. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
