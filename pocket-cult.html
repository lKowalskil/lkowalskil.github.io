/// THE DARK LOOP

Every empire runs the same six sins.

Pocket Cult is a management game wearing a villain's robe: assign workers, balance upkeep, survive events — and let the numbers grow deliciously large.

SIN—01
Build the Lair
15 buildings across 6 tiers — from a humble Altar to the lava-belching Hellgate. Level them up, unlock worker slots.
SIN—02
Summon the Horde
10 minions with real trade-offs: hire cost, meat upkeep, raid power, and per-building efficiency. Staff wisely.
SIN—03
Perform the Rituals
Tap the Ritual Stone for souls, sacrifice the weak for gold, and channel blood magic when the Eclipse hits.
SIN—04
Crush the Heroes
"Good Heroes" and holy Inquisitors will raid you. Raise defense, dispatch raids of your own, and take their gold.
SIN—05
Profit While You Sleep
True idle progress: your dungeon produces for up to 24 hours offline. Come back to a fortune. No internet required.

Ascend & Repeat
Reset the dungeon for Corruption — a permanent currency that makes every future run stronger. Deep prestige for idle veterans.
