Pocket Cult

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 13 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pocket Cult (the “App” or “Game”), published by Nyxelium (“we”, “us”, “our”), handles information when you play. Pocket Cult is an offline idle game available on Google Play (package com.nyxelium.culttycoon).

The short version
  • Your game save, progress and settings are stored only on your device. We don’t run a server that collects them, and we can’t see them.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • The Game is free and ad-supported: third-party advertising (Google AdMob) and analytics/crash reporting (Google Firebase) collect device and usage data as described below.
  • There is no account and no login.
  • You can control ad personalization through the on-screen consent prompt and your device settings.

1. Information stored on your device

Pocket Cult is designed to work offline. The following stays on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Game save & progress — your buildings, minions, resources, ascensions, achievements and overall progression.
  • Preferences — theme, language, sound, haptics and notification settings.

This data is removed when you delete the App or clear its storage. Because it never leaves your device, we cannot retrieve or restore it for you.

2. Information collected by third parties

To offer a free, ad-supported game and to keep it stable, we use the trusted third-party services below. These services may collect information directly from your device under their own privacy policies.

ServicePurposeExamples of data it may process
Google AdMob Showing banner, interstitial and rewarded ads; measuring and limiting ad frequency; fraud prevention Advertising ID, IP address (used to derive approximate location), device type and OS, app interactions, ad events
Google Firebase
(Analytics, Crashlytics & Performance)		 Understanding gameplay usage; diagnosing crashes; monitoring performance App instance identifier, device & OS info, crash logs, performance traces, in-game event counts (e.g. building purchased, ascension)

Advertising data is subject to your consent choices (see §5). Analytics, crash and performance reporting run to help us fix bugs and improve the Game.

3. How we use information

  • To provide and run the Game.
  • To display and measure advertising, including optional rewarded ads you choose to watch for in-game bonuses.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve performance and features.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

4. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where the EU/UK GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Consent — for personalized ads. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — for non-personalized ads, usage analytics, crash and performance diagnostics, security and fraud prevention, balanced against your rights.

5. Advertising & your choices

Pocket Cult shows ads via Google AdMob. Depending on your region and consent:

  • EEA, UK & Switzerland: a consent message (Google’s User Messaging Platform) is shown before personalized ads are served.
  • Reset or limit your Advertising ID: Android → Settings → Privacy → Ads (or Google → Ads), where you can reset the ID or turn off ad personalization.

Learn how Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

6. Device permissions

  • Notifications — to let you know when offline resources have piled up. You can disable them in your device settings.
  • Exact alarms — so those notifications fire at the right time.

The Game requests internet access for ads and analytics in the flows described above.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Information is processed only by the service providers named in §2, acting on our behalf or as independent controllers under their own policies, and where required by law.

8. Data retention

On-device data persists until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data held by Google services (advertising, analytics) is retained according to Google’s policies. Crash and analytics data is typically retained for a limited period and in aggregated form.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. EEA/UK under GDPR, California under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, to object to processing, to data portability, and to not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Because your game data lives only on your device, you can delete it directly by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling. For data held by Google services, see Google’s policy. To make a request to us, contact us at the address below.

10. Children’s privacy

Pocket Cult is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Ads are configured for a general audience; if you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us so we can address it.

11. International data transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than yours, including the United States. Where required, such transfers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses).

12. Security

We rely on your device’s app-sandboxing to protect on-device data and on reputable providers (Google) for processed data. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the App or on this page. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
