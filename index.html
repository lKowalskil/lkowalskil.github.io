/// THE SPEC

Every app ships against the same contract.

Six requirements, checked before every release. If a feature breaks one of them, the feature doesn't ship.

REQ—01
Offline-first
Core features work without a connection, and the content you create stays on your device.
REQ—02
No account needed
No sign-up, no login — open the app and start. Free versions show ads and use optional, consent-based analytics.
REQ—03
Zero friction
Open and go. No forced onboarding, no dark patterns, no nagging notifications.
REQ—04
Localized
Fully translated across many languages so the apps feel native wherever you are.
REQ—05
Light & fast
Small downloads, smooth performance, accessible layouts in light and dark themes.
REQ—06
Fair pricing
Free to use, with honest optional upgrades — never a wall between you and the basics.
